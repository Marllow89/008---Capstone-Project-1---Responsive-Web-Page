Oak, Ash and Thorn

Campaign

After a violent crash landing, three unlikely companions - a devout cleric named Leonnin, a skilled ranger named Cate Windrunner, and a stealthy rogue named Raven Silvarion - find themselves stranded on a remote, snow-covered island. With no clear way back, they trek through a frozen, unforgiving forest toward the small city of Spearhead Landing, hoping for refuge and answers.

But upon arrival, they quickly realize that all is not well in this isolated settlement. Children from the city have mysteriously vanished, and the desperate mayor begs the adventurers to investigate. What they uncover will reveal more than they bargained for - secrets burried in the snow, ancient forces stirring in the shadows, and a growing sense of dread that even the hardened residents of Spearhead Landing cannot escape.

As the winter winds howl and the darkness deepens, Leonnin, Cate and Raven must navigate an island where every corner could hide a clue - or a threat. Who or what is behind the disappearances? And are the answers hidden in the very land they now tread.

In this gritty, dark tale, danger lurks in unexpected places, and every decision could mean the difference between survival or doom. Trust no one, for in Spearhead Landing, the truth may be just as cold as the snow beneath your feet.
